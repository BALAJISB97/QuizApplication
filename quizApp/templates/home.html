Quiz App!

Hi Students, I welcome you to the online quiz app, here you can sharpen you knowledge by taking various quiz set available.

Read the instructions before you take the quiz!!

1. You must login to take the available quiz, if you don't have account please signup!

2. Please use your rollNumber (provided in the school) as username. Note: All the rollNumber given in the school are of 8 characters.

3. After signing, you can select the quiz you want to attend from the list of available quiz by clicking 'QuizList Tab'.

4. Every quiz is set of 10 questions. Once you've selected the quiz you will be displayed with the all the questions.

5. In the top of the quiz, your current score, total time taken and a timer will be displayed.

6. Please click the 'end' quiz button to end the quiz.

7. By clicking the 'View results' tab, you will be able to see your entire history of results.

Happy Quizzing
